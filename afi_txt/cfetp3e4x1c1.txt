Transcribing to a new CFETP is an administration function, not a re-evaluation of training.  Therefore, supervisor and trainer are considered synonymous for the purpose of training.  Transcribe within 120 days (240 days for ARC) of published date of revised CFETP.  Upon completion of a new CFETP, use the following procedures to transcribe:   2.6.1.3.1. Use the new CFTEP to identify past and current training requirements and to transcribe qualifications from previous CFETP.
