RELEASIBILITY: There are no release restrictions on this publication. __________________________________________________________________________________________    OPR:  SAF/AA Valentine Supersedes HOI 36-15, February 25, 2004 _______________________________________________________________________________________    This  Headquarters  Operating  Instruction  (HOI)  implements  Air  Force  Policy  Directive  (AFPD)  36-28  and describes  the  presentation  of  coins  by  the  Secretary,  Chief  of  Staff,  Under  Secretary,  Vice  Chief  of  Staff, Assistant  Vice  Chief  of  Staff  and  the  Chief  Master  Sergeant  of  the  Air  Force  for  recognition  of  outstanding service and contributions to the Department of the Air Force.    SUMMARY OF REVISIONS:    This  publication  has  been  revised  to  allow  the  Chief  Master  Sergeant  of  the  Air  Force  to  present  performance recognition coins.    1.  In accordance with AFI 36-2805, the Secretary, Chief of Staff, Under Secretary, Vice Chief of Staff, Assistant Vice Chief of Staff and the Chief Master Sergeant of the Air Force may present performance recognition coins for outstanding contributions or services rendered in furtherance of the Air Force mission.  Presentation of a coin award recognizes either continuing contributions or a single achievement.      2.  The Office of the Administrative Assistant to the Secretary of the Air Force (SAF/AA) will provide approval for funding all coins covered by this program for the individuals listed in the above paragraph.
