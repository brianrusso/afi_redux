CHANGE 1                CFETP 4J0X2/A                 30 April 2015 AFSC 4J0X2/A PHYSICAL MEDICINE/ORTHOTICS CAREER FIELD EDUCATION AND TRAINING PLAN  Summary: This change modifies the CFETP Part 1 and II for the 4J0X2/A. The CDC need dates have been updated. In addition, a basic life support (BLS) task has been added to the 4J0X2 STS. BLS is required of students prior to advancing to the clinical portion of the enlisted initial skills course. Lastly, the 4J0X2A Orthotics specific STS has been revised to correct tasks that were mislabeled and to include missing core tasks. This change is effective immediately.   1.
