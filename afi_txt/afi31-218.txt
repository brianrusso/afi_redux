DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE HEADQUARTERS UNITED STATES AIR FORCE WASHINGTON DC AFI31-218_AFGM2017-01  3 October 2017   MEMORANDUM  FOR  DISTRIBUTION C MAJCOMs, FOAs, DRUs  FROM: DCS/LOGISTICS, INSTALLATIONS AND MISSION SUPPORT  SUBJECT: Air Force Guidance Memorandum to AFI 31-218  1. By Order of the Secretary of the Air Force, this is an Air Force Guidance Memorandum immediately changing AFI 31-218, Motor Vehicle Traffic Supervision. Compliance with this Memorandum is mandatory. To the extent its directions are inconsistent with other Air Force publications; the information herein prevails, in accordance with AFI 33-360, Publications and Forms Management.  4-2c(1)(c) is changed to read “Refer to AFI 91-207, Paragraph 3.6.3 for a comprehensive list of required Personal Protective Equipment.”  Table 5-2, Violation #22, the words “and/or reflectorized vest” are deleted.   2. The guidance in this Memorandum becomes void after one-year from the date of the Memorandum or when superseded by a guidance publication, whichever is earlier.
