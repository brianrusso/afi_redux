The installation commander can granttemporary use of Air Force real property to others if: • The Air Force is not using the property or does not need it for military purposes now.•Such use does not interfere with the mission.• Use does not cost the Air Force much money.• Use is compatible with Air Force needs, security, and safety.1.2. Make exceptions to these priorities when it best serves the installation.1.3.2.
