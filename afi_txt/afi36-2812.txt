OPR:   SAF/MR   Certified by: SAF/MR (Ms. Sheila Earle) Pages: 3   This publication implements Air Force Policy Directive (AFPD) 36-28, Awards and Decorations Programs,  and  describes  Manpower  and  Reserve  Affairs  awards  presented  to  individuals  in recognition of their outstanding contributions or service to the Department  of the Air Force.  It explains who is eligible for the awards and applies to all Air Force military personnel, including Air  Force  Reserve  and  Air  National  Guard  personnel,  and  Air  Force  civilian  personnel.    Refer recommended  changes  and  questions  about  this  publication  to  the  Office  of  Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Forms 847 through appropriate chain of command. Manages the Manpower and Reserve Affairs Award Program. 1.2.  Establishes award criteria. 1.3. Maintains a record of all award recipients. 1.6.  Presents awards only to authorized personnel.   2  AFI36-2812  14 MARCH 2012 1.7.  Ensures awards are not  presented as personal  gifts,  tokens of  appreciation,  recognition of the contribution of unaffiliated parties. 2.  The Manpower and Reserve Affairs Recognition and Appreciation Coin Award. 2.1.  The  Assistant  Secretary  of  the  Air  Force  for  Manpower  and  Reserve  Affairs  may present  performance  recognition  coins  to  personnel  of  the  Department  of  the  Air  Force, including  the  Air  National  Guard  and  Air  Force  Reserve,  for  outstanding  contributions  or service  rendered  in  furtherance  of  the  Air  Force  mission.    Presentation  of  the  coin  award recognizes either continuing contributions or a single achievement. The purpose of this coin is to provide Manpower and Reserve Affairs with an informal means to show recognition of and appreciation for outstanding job performance.  DANIEL B.
